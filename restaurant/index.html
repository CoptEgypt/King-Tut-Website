About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

King Tut Mediterranean Restaurant began in Lynwood with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean to our community. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingredients quickly earned us a loyal following.

As we grew, we relocated to Aurora, where we continue to share our rich culinary heritage with new friends and neighbors. At King Tut, every meal is a celebration of the Mediterranean’s vibrant culture and warm hospitality.

We invite you to experience the taste and tradition that started it all.
